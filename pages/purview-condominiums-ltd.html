Purview Condominiums Ltd.

Real Estate Development, Investments & Constructions

PURVIEW CONDOMINIUMS LTD.( PCL) was incorporated in March, 1992 as a Private Limited Company and initiated business in Real Estate Development and Urban housing sector. Since 1992, the company has constructed luxury apartments in prime locations in City of Dhaka, Bangladesh and has been engaged in Construction and Development projects, Interior Decoration & Furnishings, Renovations and Project Management Services and related Consultancy.

At PCL, we have been able to set a standard for QUALITY & STYLE, which is distinctive and time tested. Our rigid quality control and professional project management style have allowed us to withstand the severe competition in construction and housing sectors. Our after sales services are remarkably sincere. Our clients are satisfied customers and they standout to be our best references.

If you have a plot of land to develop, a factory or office building to construct, an old home to refurbish, a new house to design and construct, or if you need professional help in interior design & decoration of your home, office, showroom, store or business outlet, please depend on us. We, along with our Consultants are sure to satisfy the most discerning clients.

Just call in the PCL services…!

Serving clients in Bangladesh only.
