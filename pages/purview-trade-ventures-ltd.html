PURVIEW TRADE VENTURES LTD

International Trade and Manufacturers’ Representation for active Sales

PURVIEW TRADE VENTURES LTD is International Trade and Manufacturers’ Representation operation of the Group. It also caters for trade related need of its subsidiaries. We currently specialize in sourcing of Apparel & Clothing and generic pharmaceuticals from Bangladesh. Arrangement for shipbuilding contract also undertaken. Manufacturers’ Representation (Local Agent) for proprietary sales in Bangladesh actively engaged – currently representing principals from India, U.K., Canada, USA and Malaysia. Our imports include Construction Materials, Medical & Diagnostic Equipments and Electronics.

The Company has special Interest in:

  • Manufacturers’ Representation for active sales in proprietary equipments and plants.
  • Representation in Solar Technology and Water Treatment Plants.
  • Marketing Research in Consumer Items.
  • Liaison in shipbuilding contracts.
  • Joint-venture contract on basis of Public-Private Sector Partnership.
  • Look forward to hearing from companies with ideas for business.

Serving clients in Bangladesh, Middle East, United Kingdom, European Union & all over the USA and Canada.
