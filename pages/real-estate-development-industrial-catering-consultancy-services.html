About Purview Genesis Group

International Trade Representation, Project Management, Real Estate Development, Industrial Catering & Consultancy Services

PURVIEW-GENESIS GROUP of companies have been in business since mid-80’s and expanded the businesses through early 90’s. The guiding principles on which the Group evolved are - Trust, Fidelity & Innovation. The companies/firms of the Group are involved in many aspects of International Trade and Manufacturers’ Representation, Consultancy and Project Management Services, Real Estate Development & Solutions, Industrial Catering & Support Services etc. The group is based in Dhaka, Bangladesh and also have a subsidiary in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. In the backdrop of fast paced digitization of global economy, trade & commerce, banking & finance etc., our companies are now moving ahead with the 21st Century challenges based on innovation and optimization. It’s mostly about connecting the dots in a global perspective, global positioning based on relative comparative advantages and historical knowledge of various local conditions/systems. Overall, the group management value strategic long-term partnership in most businesses and trust and fidelity is our avowed principle. E-Commerce is now on active development scheme of the Group.
